Script collection AR

Here you can find a number of scripts, using A-Frame and AR.JS for Augmented Reality


Almost all of the projects are markerbased and use the Hiro-Marker. You can find it here.

Some use the Kanji-marker

Open one of the links, allow the camera to be activated and scan the marker.

Most of the scripts are just for testing purposes and some are the works of other people with only some minor adjustments for my own goals. In that case I reference the original.
